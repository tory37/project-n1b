# Board & Map Construction

## Map Generation
- **Starting State:** The board begins empty.
- **Expansion:** Players populate hexagons starting from their closest hexagon node.
- **Connection:** Player paths eventually branch out and connect in the center.

## Tile Types
- **Spawn Points:** Characters can only be played onto specific spawn tiles.
- **Automated Tiles:** e.g., Spider Nests that spawn NPC enemies periodically.
- **Interactive Tiles:** Traps, Training grounds, or Treasure Chests.
- **Terrain Restrictions:** Different characters may have varying movement/usage rules depending on the tile type.
