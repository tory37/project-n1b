# Vision & Pillars

## Core Principles
1. **System-First Design:** The mechanics must be fun and functional without a theme (skin/theming comes later).
2. **Tactical Construction:** Players are responsible for building the map, creating a unique battlefield every game.
3. **The "North Star":** A hybrid feel of a TCG and Warhammer—pre-game preparation meets mid-game tactical execution.
