# Turn Structure & Economy

## The Tug-of-War System
Instead of a fixed action count, the turn economy is a dynamic balance.
- **The Marker:** A track shared by both players.
- **Switching Turns:**
    - If the marker moves on or past [Value: 5] on the opponent's side, the turn switches.
    - The opponent then receives those points plus a base amount.
    - Manual End: A player can end their turn early if the marker is on the opponent's side.

## Actions
- Playing Terrain/Global Cards
- Moving Entities
- Initiating Combat
- Playing Upgrades

## Currency
- **Income:** Gain X coins per turn (Base: 3).
- **Banking:** Currency persists between turns, allowing for "Big Purchases."
