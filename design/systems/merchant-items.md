# Merchant & Items

## The Market Row
The Merchant provides a selection of `[Value: 3]` items available for purchase at any time during a player's turn.

### Acquisition
- **Cost:** Each item has a Currency cost of `[Value: X]`.
- **Method:** Purchasing an item does not cost AP; it only costs Currency.
- **Usage:** Purchased items are placed into the player's **Inventory** or immediately equipped to an entity on the field.

## Item Types
Items are generic upgrades that can be applied to any compatible entity.
- **Equipment:** Weapons (Attack boost), Armor (Defense boost), or Accessories (Special abilities).
- **Consumables:** One-time use items like potions or temporary buffs.
- **[Placeholder]:** Other upgrade types to be defined (e.g., skill books, terrain modifiers).

## Restock Rules
- The market row is refilled to its capacity at the **End of Turn**.
- **Advanced Rules (Future):** The types of items that appear may be influenced by the map's terrain, Global Tiles, or specific player cards.
